About Me

I am Gabrielė Tijūnaitytė, a final year M.Sc. Geo-Information Science student at Wageningen University.

I am passionate about data science, computer vision, and their applications in addressing geospatial and environmental challenges. I enjoy solving complex problems through coding and data-driven approaches, which drives my pursuit of a career as a geospatial data scientist. I have a strong interest in applying my technical skills to the field of marine and physical oceanography.

As a student, I have gained not only valuable technical skills but also the ability to think critically, adapt, and learn quickly and efficiently. My core values include time management and timeliness, maintaining high quality, and prioritizing efficiency.

I would like to work in dynamic environments where collaboration and continuous learning drive impactful solutions.

Outside studies work, I enjoy trying out new recipies and running.
