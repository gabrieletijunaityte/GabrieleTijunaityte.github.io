About Me

I am Gabrielė Tijūnaitytė, a Research Engineer in the Artificial Intelligence group at Wageningen University, specialising in AI for Earth Observations.

With a strong foundation in Geo-Information Science, my work lies at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, and geospatial analysis. I am dedicated to building impactful, data-driven solutions to environmental challenges. My primary expertise involves working with multi-modal satellite imagery, large vision-language models, and contrastive deep learning frameworks.

Outside work, I enjoy trying out new recipes and running.
